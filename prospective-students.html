To Prospective Students:

I usually look for one or two self-motivated PhD students each year (starting in Spring or Fall) to work on challenging problems in computer vision, such as visual tracking (in 2D videos or point clouds), video object detection, segmentation, visual attack and defense and other related areas. Qualified students will be supported by research or teaching assistantships. If you'd like to know my research work, please refer to my piblications at this page.

If interested, prospective students are encouraged to contact me (my e-mail is heng.fan@unt.edu) with your full CV, transcripts and any other materials that you think can help me better know you.

Application deadlines:

  • Spring: September 15th

  • Fall: January 15th

Note: GRE could be WAIVED for excellent applicants.

In addition to PhD students, motivated master and undergraduate students within UNT are also welcome. But note that, in general, my group does not provide financial support for master or undergraduate students. If you're interested in conducting reseach with me, please contact me.
