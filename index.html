Short Bio

Heng Fan is currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of North Texas. He received his B.S. from Huazhong Agricultural University in 2013 and Ph.D. (advised by Professor Haibin Ling) from Stony Brook University in 2021, respectively. His research interests include computer vision, robotics, and medical image analysis. He has served as an Area Chair for WACV 2022.

To prospective students: I am looking for multiple self-motivated PhD students in 2022/2023 to work on challenging problems (e.g., visual object tracking, video object detection/segmentation) in computer vision. Please send me your CV and transcripts if you are interested. Motivated master and undergraduate students within UNT are also welcome.
