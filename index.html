Short Bio

I am a PhD student in Department of Computer Science, Stony Brook University, advised by Prof. Haibin Ling. Prior to this, I was a PhD student at Temple University. I spent two and a half years as a master student (courses completed) in Huazhong Agricultural University, where I received my B.S. degree in 2013.
